## Dynamic Pricing Strategies

AI-powered dynamic pricing engines analyze market conditions, competitor pricing, and demand patterns in real-time to optimize pricing strategies.

```python
# Example: Simple price optimization model
from sklearn.ensemble import RandomForestRegressor

def optimize_price(demand_factors, competitor_prices, historical_data):
    model = RandomForestRegressor()
    model.fit(historical_data['features'], historical_data['revenue'])
    
    optimal_price = model.predict([
        demand_factors + competitor_prices
    ])
    
    return optimal_price
```
